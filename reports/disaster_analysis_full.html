🌍 Global Disaster Analytics Report

Analysis of Natural Disasters (1900-2024)

Author: Demian Pyrih | October 2025

📊 Summary

3,495 disasters analyzed | 713,927 deaths | 101.9M people affected | $56.7B damage

🔥 Key Findings

  • Disaster frequency shows significant variation across decades
  • Asia experiences 58% of all global disasters
  • Economic damages correlate with time (r=0.02)
  • Machine Learning models identify patterns in disaster types
  • Average 204 deaths per disaster event
